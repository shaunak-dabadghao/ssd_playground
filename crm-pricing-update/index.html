Why Prices Changed

  • Oct 2025 price drop: Post-disruption model retrain shifted risk mix to higher groups, lowering CDRs for RG 3-6.
  • Nov 2025 hold: Second disruption reversed the shift, but we held prices to maintain adoption momentum. ~30 bps ALM impact was acceptable.
  • Jan 2026 increase: GRM 50-50 rollout requires accurate CRM baseline for fair conversion comparison between models.
